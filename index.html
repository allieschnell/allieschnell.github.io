Fond du Lads

What are the Fond du Lads?

The Fond du Lads is an all-male double quartet from Fond du Lac High School. We're a rollicking band of singers who would be delighted to perform for you. We'll sing for anything that you need, be it background music at a wedding reception, entertainment at an otherwise-boring office party, or even a personal show for a birthday! See our Contact page for details on how to get a hold of us.
